This a Pomodoro Timer, and its based on the princple of the Pomodoro Technique. The general idea of it is to work for 25 minutes, then when the clock hits 0, you press the break button and take a break for 5 minutes. During those 5 minutes, you should do something like walk around, get something to drink, or grab a snack. And once the 5 minutes are up, click on the start button again to reset the timer to 25 minutes. There will be a beep everytime the timers reaches 0, so don't be to suprised when you here it.
